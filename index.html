Our Story

We are a nonprofit company that sells hand-made bird houses for charity! All profits are donated to charities devoted to protecting the environment and saving animal lives.

FAQ

Who makes the birdhouses?
The birdhouses are hand-made and handpainted by volunteering woodworkers and artists, including our founders.
Is Chirp for Charities international?
Currently, we only ship within the US and we don’t ship to Hawaii and Alaska. We hope to change this in the future.
Does Chirp for Charites accept returns/exchanges?
We don't except returns, but if your birdhouse is damaged upon receiving it or the wrong size, then we can exchange it for a new one. Disclaimer: A replacement of the same size and design might not be available and you may have to choose another birdhouse of the same value
I’m a painter and/or woodworker. How can I help?
Read our quality standards guide. Then, contact us at jcarter@chirpforcharity.com for next steps. Be sure to include your name or alias, the state you live in, and whether you intend to build and/or paint.
